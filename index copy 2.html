About the Custom PACT

Welcome to the official platform of Custom PACT, a transformative initiative powered by Nigeria Customs Service (NCS) in collaboration with the African Continental Free Trade Area (AfCFTA) and supported by the African Export-Import Bank (AFREXIMBANK).

This visionary project exemplifies our unwavering commitment to revolutionize customs operations, promote intra-african trade, and position Africa as a formidable force in global trade. Inspired by a bold vision to create a unified, efficient, and secure trade environment, Custom PACT aims to set a new standard for cross-border cooperation and trade facilitation.

Our mission is unequivocally to establish a strong, transparent, and agile customs framework that promotes lawful trade, enhances security, and drives sustainable economic growth. We are determined to lead this monumental transformation, harnessing technology, collaboration, and strategic vision.

Together, we are forging a future in transforming customs collaboration, trade facilitation across Africa and building a prosperous future for our nations.

Join us in this historic journey.

Bashir Adewale Adeniyi (MFR), PhD
Comptroller General
Nigeria Customs Service

Visa Information

We facilitate visa processing for all international delegates. Please review the requirements below and contact our team for assistance.

  • ECOWAS citizens: Visa-free entry to Nigeria
  • Online visa application available at immigration.gov.ng
  • Yellow fever vaccination certificate required
  • Invitation letters provided upon registration
  • Processing time: 5-7 business days
  • Visa on arrival available for select countries

Arrival and transfer

We facilitate visa processing for all international delegates. Please review the requirements below and contact our team for assistance.

  • The organizing committee will welcome participants at Nnamdi Azikiwe International Airport in Abuja and ensure their transfer to hotels
  • The organizing committee will only provide transportation for delegates from the hotels to the meeting venue and vice versa

Travel Information

Abuja is well-connected internationally. We provide comprehensive travel support to ensure your smooth arrival and departure.

  • Nnamdi Azikiwe International Airport (ABV)
  • Multiple international airlines serve Abuja daily
  • Complimentary airport transfers for delegates
  • Daily shuttle service between venues
  • 30 minutes drive from airport to city center
  • VIP pickup service available upon request
