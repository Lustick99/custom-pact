Visa Information

We facilitate visa processing for all international delegates. Please review the requirements below and contact our team for assistance.

  • Online visa application available at immigration.gov.ng
  • Yellow fever vaccination certificate required
  • Invitation letters provided upon registration
  • Processing time: 5-7 business days
  • Visa on arrival available for select countries
