About the Customs PACT

Welcome to the official platform of Customs PACT, a transformative initiative powered by Nigeria Customs Service (NCS) in collaboration with the African Continental Free Trade Area (AfCFTA) and supported by the African Export-Import Bank (AFREXIMBANK) and the World Customs Organization (WCO).

This visionary project exemplifies our commitment to revolutionize customs and business cooperation, promote intra-african trade, and position Africa as a formidable force in global trade. Inspired by a bold vision to create a unified, efficient, and secure trade environment, Customs PACT aims to set a new standard for cross-border cooperation and trade facilitation.

Our mission is unequivocally to establish a strong, transparent, and agile customs framework that promotes lawful trade, enhances security, and drives sustainable economic growth. We are determined to lead this monumental transformation, harnessing technology, collaboration, and strategic vision.

Together, we are forging a future in transforming customs collaboration, trade facilitation across Africa and building a prosperous future for our nations.

Join us in this historic journey.
