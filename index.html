About The Customs PACT

Transforming African Trade Through Partnership

Our Mission

The Customs PACT brings together African customs administrations, trade organizations, and stakeholders to foster cooperation, eliminate trade barriers, and build sustainable bridges for economic growth across the continent.

Event Logistics

Everything You Need to Know

Visa Information

We facilitate visa processing for all international delegates. Please review the requirements below and contact our team for assistance.

  • ECOWAS citizens: Visa-free entry to Nigeria
  • Online visa application available at immigration.gov.ng
  • Yellow fever vaccination certificate required
  • Invitation letters provided upon registration
  • Processing time: 5-7 business days
  • Visa on arrival available for select countries

Travel Information

Abuja is well-connected internationally. We provide comprehensive travel support to ensure your smooth arrival and departure.

  • Nnamdi Azikiwe International Airport (ABV)
  • Multiple international airlines serve Abuja daily
  • Complimentary airport transfers for delegates
  • Daily shuttle service between venues
  • 30 minutes drive from airport to city center
  • VIP pickup service available upon request

Accommodation Options

We've partnered with premium hotels to offer exclusive rates for event delegates. Book early to secure the best prices.

  • Transcorp Hilton Abuja - Main conference venue
  • Abuja Continental Hotel - 5-star partner hotel
  • Fraser Suites Abuja - Premium serviced apartments
  • Special conference rates with up to 25% discount
  • Complimentary breakfast included for delegates
  • Free WiFi and business center access

Conference Services

We provide world-class conference facilities and services to ensure a productive and comfortable experience for all participants.

  • Simultaneous interpretation in English, French & Arabic
  • High-speed WiFi connectivity throughout venues
  • Daily coffee breaks and gourmet lunch service

  • Networking lounge and business center facilities
  • Mobile event app for schedules and networking
